Splash Rafters

Splash Rafters exists to create safe, fun, and unforgettable river adventures. Our mission is to guide every guest with skill and care, turning each trip into a meaningful experience. Our creed: paddle with integrity, protect the waters, and treat every rafter like family. Motto: “Ride the River, Share the Joy.”

History

Our rafting company began as a small dream shared by friends who loved the river. Starting with borrowed paddles and weekend trips, word spread about our care and safety. Over the years, we expanded routes, trained skilled guides, and welcomed adventurers from everywhere. Our heart remains the same: a passion for the water and helping others experience it safely and joyfully.
